# 🗳️ Decoding the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election
### Resume Project Challenge by Codebasics

## 📌 Project Overview
This project was created as part of the **Codebasics Resume Project Challenge (RPC)** titled:

> **"Decoding the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election"**

The challenge focused on transforming raw Election Commission of India (ECI) data into a storytelling-driven analytics presentation for **AtliQ Media**, a fictional national news network.

The objective was not prediction or political commentary, but rather:
- extracting meaningful insights,
- building compelling visual stories,
- and presenting election trends in a neutral, fact-based manner.

This project combines:
- Data Analytics
- Data Storytelling
- Power BI Dashboarding
- Election Data Visualization
- Editorial-style Insight Presentation

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement
AtliQ Media wanted to create a one-hour election results show based entirely on public Election Commission data — without political debates, predictions, or opinions.

As a freelance Data Analyst, my responsibility was to:
- analyze the 2026 Tamil Nadu Assembly Election results,
- identify the most interesting electoral stories,
- and present them in a simple, engaging, and neutral format suitable for television storytelling.

The challenge emphasized:
- storytelling over complex analysis,
- clean visualization over clutter,
- and neutrality over political interpretation.

---

# 📊 Selected Research Stories
Instead of answering every research question, this project focuses deeply on **3 major election stories**:

## 1️⃣ The Flip Story
Analyzing constituencies that changed winning parties between:
- Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2021
- Tamil Nadu Assembly Election 2026

### Key Focus:
- Which parties gained the most flipped seats?
- Which regions experienced the biggest electoral shifts?
- Seat retention vs constituency turnover

---

## 2️⃣ Vote Share Story
Understanding how vote share changed across major political parties.

### Key Focus:
- Party-wise vote share comparison
- Growth and decline patterns
- Difference between seat share and vote share

---

## 3️⃣ Margin Story
Exploring victory margins across constituencies.

### Key Focus:
- Closest contests
- Landslide victories
- Highly competitive constituencies
- Distribution of winning margins
